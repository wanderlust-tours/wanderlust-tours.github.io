About Us

Welcome to Wanderlust Tours 🌍

Where every journey is more than just travel — it's an unforgettable experience of history, spirituality, and adventure.

Saurabh Amble · Founder

I'm Saurabh Amble, the proud owner and tour planner behind Wanderlust Tours. With a deep passion for exploring India's rich heritage and divine destinations, I personally design each tour to give you a meaningful and memorable experience.

At Wanderlust Tours, we specialize in:

🏯 Exploring the legendary forts of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj
⛩️ Visiting ancient and sacred temples
🏛️ Discovering heritage places full of history
🕉️ Experiencing cultural & spiritual destinations

Every trip is carefully planned to give you the perfect blend of history + adventure + devotion.
Whether you are traveling solo or with a group, we also offer customized tours based on your preferences — because your journey should be as unique as you are.
